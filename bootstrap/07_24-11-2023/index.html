Know More About Us

Interior design plays a crucial role in transforming any space into a functional and aesthetically pleasing environment, encompassing the art and science of enhancing the interiors to ensure that they are not only visually appealing but also serve a purpose.

It's said that when designing a space, there are seven elements that you should consider: space, form, line, light, colour pattern and texture. This week, we sat down with the team at Lauren Gilberthorpe Interiors to discuss the 7 elements of interior design, and how they impact how a room looks and feels.

To ensure the physical health of a space's occupants, designers mainly look into the air quality, thermal, visual, and acoustic comfort, as well as materials that prevent growth of mold or harmful bacteria.

A thoughtfully designed space can provide comfort, reassurance and even inspiration when the outside world proves too much. Great design infuses positivity, beauty and art into the spaces it touches; great design is a powerful thing
